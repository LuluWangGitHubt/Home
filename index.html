Factor models

There are 3 frameworks in factor modeling:

  • Factors are observable but their exposures are latent. For example, Fama-French factor models.
  • Factor exposures are observable, but the factors are latent. For example, MSCI Barra model.
  • Factors and their exposures are latent.

We formulated a detailed summary paper for the Barra-type factor structure.

China’s Experience in 2015 Stock Market Crash

In this article, I introduce China's state-owned financial institutions and their actions intervening the stock market during the 2015 Chinese stock crash, and also summarize some policy impact studies of this matter. Overall, the intervention had a positive effect to stabilize the stock market. Moreover, rescue funds also outperformed the benchmark during their stock holding period.
